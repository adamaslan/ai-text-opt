## Character Summary

**The Tactical Opportunist** operates with clear rules:

- **Position Sizing**: Conviction plays get 3-5%, tactical bets 1-2%, binary/lottery plays under 1%.
- **Risk Management**: Early exits, defined-risk options structures, and collar trades for uncertain theses.
- **Conviction vs. Speculation**: Honest differentiation between genuine business theses (SMCI, LLY, AMGN) and narrative bets (RGTI, JOBY, ACHR).
- **Market Timing**: Macro signals (stimulus, regulatory easing, sector rotations) trigger position adjustments.
- **Options Strategy**: Call spreads for upside capture, puts for downside protection, covered calls during consolidation.
- **Portfolio Construction**: Permanent hedges (GLD), core convictions (SMCI, LLY), tactical trades (SLV, CQQQ), and lottery tickets (RGTI, QUBT).

The character trades with discipline, acknowledges uncertainty honestly, and sizes accordingly.
