# T2 (Structured Growth Investor) - 100 Deep-Dive Questions

## Profile Overview

**T2 Profile Characteristics:**
- **Holding Period:** 1-10 years (long-term strategic positions)
- **Primary Strategy:** Trend-following + sector tailwinds (Axiom III focus)
- **Tax Strategy:** Long-term capital gains, lower tax drag base
- **Core Edge:** Structural momentum, sector rotation, compounding through multiple market cycles
- **Risk Model:** Fixed fractional on larger accounts; growth-adjusted position sizing

## Summary: T2 Trader Development Path

### Quick Checklist for T2 Investors

- [ ] **Defined multi-year thesis** for each major position (3-5 years minimum)
- [ ] **Quantified fundamental growth drivers** (revenue, EPS, margins, ROE targets)
- [ ] **Identified sector tailwinds** supporting each position for 5+ years
- [ ] **Backtested trend-following criteria** for long-term holdings (higher highs/lows, 200-MA relationship)
- [ ] **Calculated LTCG tax benefit** of holding >365 days vs. selling earlier
- [ ] **Built sector rotation thesis** for next 3-5 years
- [ ] **Established rebalancing discipline** (annual, quarterly, or trigger-based)
- [ ] **Defined position concentration limits** (max % per position, max % per sector)
- [ ] **Created loss-harvesting plan** for long-term positions
- [ ] **Set conviction degradation thresholds** (when to reduce/exit positions)

### Key Metrics to Track

1. **Thesis Validation Rate**: % of theses that deliver expected outcomes (growth, sector performance, trend continuation)
2. **Conviction Degradation**: How conviction changes over time; when does it drop below "hold" threshold?
3. **Tax Efficiency**: Average holding period, % of gains as LTCG vs. STCG
4. **Concentration Drift**: How often do positions drift to >20% of portfolio? How do you rebalance?
5. **Sector Rotation Timing**: How well do you time sector entry/exit? How many rotations per 5-year cycle?
6. **Compounding Return**: CAGR over 5-10 year holding periods; how does this compare to SPY?
7. **Drawdown Recovery**: After a 20-30% portfolio decline, how long to recover to previous high?
8. **Thesis Failure Rate**: How many positions are exited due to "broken thesis" vs. "profit target"?

### Performance Attribution

For a T2 investor, break returns into:
1. **Sector allocation** (which sectors did you overweight/underweight?)
2. **Stock selection within sectors** (did you pick the best performers?)
3. **Buy-and-hold compounding** (did you benefit from long holding periods?)
4. **Tax efficiency** (did LTCG treatment improve after-tax returns?)
5. **Rebalancing** (did disciplined rebalancing add value?)
